Frequently Asked Questions.
At AutoBrilliance, I'm happy to answer any questions you may have.

Are you insured?

Yes, David has a comprehensive insurance policy in place to cover public liability, personal accident, moving of your vehicle and damage to goods.


Where do the treatments take place?

All treatments can be carried out at David’s studio or he can visit you, which ever you prefer. If he comes to you then he will need the use of an outside tap, a source of electricity and shelter from the weather. If you don’t have a garage or car port, for example, then David will need to work on a driveway but will obviously be governed by the weather conditions.


What if I want a treatment not listed, or only need specific parts of any one treatment?

A bespoke package can easily be created and tailored to your specific needs. Make contact now to discuss your specific requirements and book your free assessment.


Do I have to have a new or expensive car to book an appointment?

Certainly not! David welcomes contact from all clients, irrespective of the cost, make or age of your car. David’s intention is to provide his clients with fantastic service and to apply the most appropriate treatment in order to transform the look of your car, whilst adding value or protecting your investment.
